Curriculum

This page serves as a comprehensive resource hub for astronomy enthusiasts and educators, offering a wide array of links to valuable content. In the "Links" section, visitors can explore daily and archived images of the cosmos, stunning photographs of celestial bodies like Pluto, and the latest solar observations from NASA's SOHO and SDO missions. Additionally, the "Astronomy K-12 Education" section provides educational tools and interactive experiences, such as the Scale of the Universe, daily moon guides, and insights into the role of data science and AI in space exploration. Whether you're a student, teacher, or simply fascinated by the universe, this page connects you to a wealth of knowledge and captivating visuals to fuel your curiosity about space.
